Career Goals

Our career goals are focused on continuous growth, innovation, and contributing to the tech community by pushing the boundaries of web development.

  • Become experts in Web Development: Master front-end and back-end technologies to create intuitive, responsive, and dynamic websites and applications.
  • Excel in User Experience (UX) Design: Build user-centric designs that not only look great but also provide seamless navigation and exceptional functionality.
  • Develop Innovative Web Applications: Continuously push the envelope by creating unique, scalable web applications that solve real-world problems and enhance user engagement.
  • Contribute to Open-Source Projects: Actively participate in and contribute to global open-source web development projects, sharing knowledge and fostering collaboration within the tech community.

Project Ideas

Our future project ideas include:

Member Project Idea Description
JIANNA Recipe Recommender An app that suggests recipes based on ingredients the user inputs. Due to dietary restrictions, most importantly allergies, the app also features filters that exclude certain ingredients from the suggestions loaded. Recipes may not only be limited to the ingredients given by the user. Users have a lot of freedom in personalizing the recipes they seek in the app through settings they can configure.
ATHEENA AI Budget Tracker An app that helps its users track expenses and savings, but with a twist. The app integrates the use of AI to help and give suggestions to users on how to allocate income, what goals to set, and what bills to pay. It would also give warnings when the user is predicted to exceed the budget and will make use of visual representations like pie graphs or charts for data summaries.
JEDIDIAH Mr. DIY App An educational and user-friendly platform designed to simplify shopping with Mr. DIY. Explore diverse product categories, from home improvement tools to electronics and household essentials, while learning about their features. The app ensures a seamless, convenient shopping experience right at your fingertips.
KAINA E-Commerce Management App for Small Businesses An app that helps small businesses simplify their day-to-day operations by efficiently tracking sales, inventory; managing orders, and providing automated reports, invoices, receipts; and give business insights by providing analytics and suggestions.
MICHAEL Recipe Recommender An app that helps tourists and commuters in Cebu navigate the jeepney routes efficiently. This app provides detailed information about jeepney codes, estimated fare expenses, and real-time tracking in order to make travelling more convenient. Commuting through ride-hailing services can often be expensive at times and we wanted to spend less on transportation costs but we have no idea the different modes and routes of transportation especially for first timers in Cebu. With this app, users can find the best jeepney routes to ride based on their inputted starting point and destination, with a visual map integration to display the route, along with calculated estimated fares.
